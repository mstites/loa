Project Overview

My primary project during my LOA is to design and make sex toys with trans feminine pleasure specifically in mind. This project is part of a greater dream of mine to have sex toys which are truly genital and gender neutral.

By the end of the semester, my MVP (minimial viable product) is to have a fully functional prototype design. My stretch goal is to have a sellable product that can be manufactured at a small scale.

Supporting my Work

If you are interested in supporting this project and have the financial means, please venmo money to @MaeveStites with "LOA" in the description.

The money will go towards buying supplies and samples, making prototypes, and hopefully (if I get to this point) making the product accessible to trans people at an affordable price. Currently, all of the money for this project is coming from my personal savings.

Outside of monetary donations, I am always in need of the support and skills of others. If you have any skills, knowledge, or perspectives you would like to give to this project please reach out. More information on the contact page.
