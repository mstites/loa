Project Overview

The primary project during my LOA is to design and prototype sex toys with trans feminine pleasure at the forefront. This project is part of a long term interest I have had in gender affirming sex and pleasure. I am a certified sex nerd :).

Why is this important?

In the current adult market very little consideration is paid to trans pleasure while designing and marketing sex toys. This is especially true for trans feminine people who sit at the bottom of the pleasure power sturcture due to the intersections of transphobia and misogyny.

My work will focus on toys that are pleasurable for trans feminine people with penises. The existing toys for penises on the market are entirely designed for cis men. This is problematic for many trans feminine people for two reasons:

  1. The ways the toys are designed to be used is not affirming and/or dysphoria inducing. Strokers are a common toy for cis men that can be really uncomfortable for trans feminine people to use.
  2. Many trans feminine people take hormones (HRT). HRT can cause a number of changes in the penis (eg higher sensitivity, softer, less/no erections) which can make toys designed for cis men's penises not pleasurable and even painful to use.

This leaves transfeminine people stuck with penetrative toys (eg dildos, plugs) or toys designed for vulvas when looking for affirming pleasure. Penetrative toys are great (and the great equalizer)! But transfeminine people deserve access to toys for affirming and pleasurable penile (analogous to clitoral) simulation. This is true even for trans feminine people who are content with sex toys designed for cis men. There are always more things to explore!

Project Outcome

Deliverable

The final goal of my project is to have a working, refined, prototype (or prototypes). In the future, I would like to work towards a marketable, sellable, product.

Learning Goals

The big goal of this project is personal growth and learning. My focus areas for learning are:

  • Self-directed learning and project management skills
  • Using creative, efficient, and effective (goals orientated) prototyping methods
  • Making body safe silicone toys
  • The idea to product pipeline
  • The intersection of art and engineering
  • Queer and trans sexuality and pleasure
