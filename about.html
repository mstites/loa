My Transness & Sex Nerd Mind

Sexuality

I have had a long battle with my personal relationships around my body, sex, and feeling grounded on the earth. The longer I have been going through the earth and learning more about the expansiveness of gender, bodies, and sex - the more connected I have become with my own body. A few years ago, I felt completely disconnected from my body, to the point where it consumed my thoughts and distracted e fromm my every day life - making every day a drudging pain to get through. Today, my body and mine feel one - and I believe thinking about our bodies in a sexual and physical context is an important part of that.

I think there are a lot of narratives around being trans and having sex that I internalized early on. I always had the idea that I could never have good sex. I always thought I would eternally hate my body, both in sex and just navigating the world day to day. ANd in internalizing those thoughts, I turned what was already hard into something seemingly impossible to overcome. Through a fourtunate turn of events, I was given access to community and loving friends andpartners who helped me to give the space I needed to heal from the messenging and society which did not love or represent bodies like mine.

I want to be a part of brining pleasure, grounding, connection, and beauty to the center of trans - specifically trans feminine - bodies.
